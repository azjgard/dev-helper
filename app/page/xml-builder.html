This tool is designed to be a simple aid in Courseware Development. As you navigate through a lesson on the site http://avondale.iol you'll be prompted on each slide to indicate the matching design model.

After making your selection, this page will be instantly populated with an editable element containing XML that has been populated with the text from the slide.

After navigating through an entire lesson, you can click the "Export Content" below button to get all of the generated XML copied onto your clipboard.
